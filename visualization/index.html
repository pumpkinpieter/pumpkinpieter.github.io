Waveguide Field Visualization

Depicted below is the field in a simple slab waveguide resulting from the input of a truncated parabolic signal.
The device is composed of 3 layers: a high index core region and two lower index cladding region.
The input end of the guide is initially closest to the viewer.

Left click on image to start/stop animation.
Use scroll wheel (or two finger swipe) to zoom in/out.
Left click and drag to rotate image.
Right click and drag to translate image
